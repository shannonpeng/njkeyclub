Roles & responsibilities.

Roles of a Webmaster

  • Create, maintain, and update a club website.
  • Utilize social media to aid in publicizing.
  • Upload any digital forms/content to make available online for others to print or download.
  • Provide any technical assistance.
  • Participate in events & community service - after all, we're in Key Club!

Webmasters in the Big Picture

The Webmaster is vital to any Key Club in our technological world because he/she:

  • is usually the go-to person for any technical assistance.
  • helps to connect the club through technology such as social media (and the website, of course.)
  • allows the whole Key Club to function more efficiently as significant forms and other resources can be digitized and available for convenience.

Development Methods

Novice Advanced
+ Pros + Pros
  • Easy, quick to create and maintain
  • Built-in features and widgets
  • Plenty of layouts, themes and other options to choose from
  • Very customizable - make your site look and function pretty much exactly how you imagine it
  • Pride in your accomplishments
- Cons - Cons
  • Not as customizable
  • Might have restrictions on page limits and more
  • Companies may impose their branding (including logos) and unwanted ads on your site
  • You'll need a domain & hosting - most of the time this won't be free
  • (Small) learning curve
