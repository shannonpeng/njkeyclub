District Convention 2015

Important Documents

Download the registration packet and flyers below to get ready for District Convention 2015!

What is District Convention?

District Convention is an amazing opportunity to meet other Key Clubbers within the state of New Jersey!

You will have the chance to attend insightful workshops led by your District Board while having fun with other Key Clubbers at a beachside hotel!

When and where is the convention?

This year's District Convention will be held from March 27th, 2015 to March 29th, 2015 at Ocean Place Resort & Spa, Long Branch, NJ!

What is the cost?

All registration forms must be postmarked by Friday, March 6th, 2015!

The prices are: $270 for Key Clubbers and $285 for chaperones (double occupancy room) or $350 for chaperones (single occupancy room).

If your club registers prior to Friday, February 13th, 2015, you may receive a deduction of $5 per person. Registrations (check or school voucher) postmarked after February 13th CANNOT take the $5 discount.
