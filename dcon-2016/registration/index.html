Registration

Registration for DCON 2016 is $285 per member, $325 for chaperones (double occupancy room), or $430 for chaperones (single occupancy room). Clubs that register before the early deadline of February 12, 2016 will receive a $5 discount per member.

Click the button below to download the registration packet, including all required forms. For any questions or concerns, contact District Treasurer Bobbie Boettinger at treasurer.boettinger@gmail.com.
