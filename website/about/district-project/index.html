District Project: ELIMINATE

The New Jersey District of Key Club International is proud to announce that we have chosen the Eliminate Project as our District Project for the 2014-2015 service year.

With The Eliminate Project, Kiwanis International and UNICEF have teamed up to rid the world of maternal and neonatal tetanus. Neonatal tetanus kills one baby every nine minutes, that's nearly 60,000 newborns a year. MNT causes excruciating pain to newborns, while the mother can only stand nearby and watch their baby cry out in pain.

To eliminate maternal and neonatal tetanus from the Earth, 129 million mothers and babies must be immunized. To cover the cost of vaccines, syringes, and other costs, the Kiwanis Family and UNICEF will need to raise by 2015:

$110 Million
