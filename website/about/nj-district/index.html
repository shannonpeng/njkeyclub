New Jersey District

Our district is one of the best Key Club districts in the world (in our opinion). It is comprised of 22 divisions across New Jersey.

Governed by the New Jersey District Board of Trustees

Each year, a new District Board is elected. The board is composed of 22 Lieutenant Governors, one for each division. Lieutenant Govenors are elected in January and February by the clubs in their division. Get to know yours today! The Governor, Secretary, Treasurer, and Editor are elected at the House of Delegates at District Convention in April. Finally, the District Webmaster is appointed each year to make njkeyclub.org possible!
