Fall Rally

Important Documents

Download the flyer and registration packet below to get ready for Fall Rally 2014!

What is Fall Rally?

Fall Rally is the largest gathering of Key Clubbers in New Jersey to motivate and energize Key Clubbers to achieve greater service in the upcoming year!

Why should I go?

You'll get to meet new friends, listen to your District Board speak about important service year updates, and last but not least, have fun at Six Flags!

What is this year's theme?

This year's theme is animation! Come dressed in your favorite animated character, including Spongebob, Scooby Doo, and more!

When and where is it?

This year's Fall Rally will be held on October 12th, 2014 at Six Flags Great Adventure!

Buses will arrive between 7:30AM and 8:30AM and registration will take place between 7:30AM and 9:15AM!

How much will it cost?

Early registration costs $37 and must be completed by September 24th, 2014. Late registration costs $41 and must be completed before Fall Rally begins! The event costs $6 for season passholders!
